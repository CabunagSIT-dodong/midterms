My Favorite Movies

  • Inception
  • The Matrix

Inception

A mind-bending thriller where a skilled thief enters people's dreams to steal secrets, but takes on the challenge of planting an idea instead. Directed by Christopher Nolan, this film explores layers of dreams within dreams.

The Matrix

A revolutionary sci-fi movie depicting a dystopian future where humanity is trapped inside a simulated reality, and a group of rebels fights to uncover the truth. Directed by the Wachowskis, a defining cyberpunk classic.

Interstellar

Explores space travel, black holes, and time dilation as a team of astronauts search for a new home for humanity. Another Nolan masterpiece, it combines emotional storytelling with stunning visuals and scientific concepts.
